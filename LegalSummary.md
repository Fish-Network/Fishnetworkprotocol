# 🐟 Fish Points
## Legal Evaluation Summary

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Fish Points are a **non-transferable, on-chain reputation scoring system** used within the Fish Network platform to measure user participation and engagement in investment-related activities.

They are:

- not transferable  
- not redeemable  
- not convertible to cash or assets  
- not tied to profits or economic rights  

They function as:

> **a reputation and access-control mechanism, not a financial instrument.**

---

## 2. Core Characteristics

### 2.1 Non-Financial Nature

Fish Points:

- do **not represent ownership** in any entity  
- do **not represent a claim on assets or cash flows**  
- do **not entitle holders to dividends, yield, or profit sharing**  
- do **not appreciate based on market demand**  

They are strictly:

> **a score reflecting user activity and participation**

---

### 2.2 Non-Transferability

- Users **cannot transfer, sell, or trade** Fish Points  
- No secondary market exists or is permitted  

Smart contracts explicitly disable:
- `transfer`  
- `transferFrom`  
- `approve`  

This prevents:
- speculation  
- price discovery  
- financialization  

---

### 2.3 No Redemption Mechanism

Fish Points:

- cannot be redeemed for:
  - cash  
  - tokens  
  - equity  
  - services (in a fixed or guaranteed way)  

They may influence:
- access  
- prioritization  
- participation  

But **do not function as consideration or payment**

---

### 2.4 Wallet-Bound Identity

- Fish Points are tied to a **wallet address**
- They represent **user behavior within the platform**  

---

## 3. Functional Use Cases

Fish Points are used internally within the platform for:

### 3.1 Reputation Signaling
- ranking participants  
- identifying active contributors  

### 3.2 Access Control
- gating features  
- prioritizing participation in opportunities  

### 3.3 Governance Weighting (Limited)
- may influence voting weight  
- does not confer ownership or control rights  

---

## 4. Scoring Model

Fish Points are calculated as:

FP(total) = FP(capital) + FP(participation)


### FP_capital
- reflects capital-related participation (e.g., deposits)

### FP_participation
- reflects non-financial contributions:
  - sourcing  
  - diligence  
  - research  
  - voting  
  - operational actions  

All scoring is:

- internally defined  
- non-financial  
- not tied to profit outcomes  

---

## 5. Absence of Investment Characteristics

Fish Points are **not investments** because they lack:

| Investment Characteristic | Present? |
|--------------------------|----------|
| Expectation of profit | ❌ |
| Transferability | ❌ |
| Secondary market | ❌ |
| Revenue rights | ❌ |
| Ownership rights | ❌ |
| Dividend or yield | ❌ |
| Capital appreciation mechanism | ❌ |

---

## 6. Relationship to Fish Schools

Fish Schools are:

- Wyoming DAO LLC investment clubs  
- separate legal entities  

Fish Points:

- do **not represent membership interests**  
- do **not represent equity**  
- do **not impact legal ownership**  

They are:

> **a platform-level scoring mechanism only**

---

## 7. Data & Evidence Model (Unredeemable)

Unlike the redeemable template:

- no formal attestation registry exists  
- no formal verification of financial outcomes is required  
- scoring is **simplified and system-driven**  

This reduces:
- regulatory exposure  
- reliance on financial claims  
- need for audit-grade validation  

---

## 8. Compliance Positioning

Fish Points (Unredeemable) are designed to be:

- **non-security**  
- **non-commodity**  
- **non-payment instrument**  
- **non-stored value system**  

They are most analogous to:

- loyalty points (without redemption)  
- reputation scores  
- in-platform engagement metrics  

---

## 9. Risk Considerations

### 9.1 Potential Misinterpretation Risk

Users may perceive:

- higher Fish Points → better financial outcomes  

Mitigation:

- clear disclaimers  
- no performance promises  
- no return-based scoring in unredeemable template  

---

### 9.2 Indirect Financial Influence

Fish Points may:

- influence access to deals  

Mitigation:

- ensure FP does not guarantee allocation  
- maintain discretionary allocation systems  

---

### 9.3 Regulatory Recharacterization Risk

Authorities could evaluate:

- whether FP indirectly influences economic opportunity  

Mitigation:

- maintain strict non-transferability  
- avoid linking FP to financial rewards  

---

## 10. Summary Statement

Fish Points (Unredeemable) are:

> **a non-transferable, non-financial, on-chain reputation score used solely for participation, ranking, and access within the Fish Network platform.**

They:

- do not represent an investment  
- do not provide economic rights  
- do not enable speculation  
- do not function as a financial instrument
